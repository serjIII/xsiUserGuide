Hair Resample Operator Property Editor

Resamples hair after styling or deforming in Stretchy mode (see Stretching Hair Segments [ Hair ]) by averaging out the stretched hair segments over the whole hair strand, giving the hair a more uniform appearance.

To apply: Style or deform hair in Stretchy mode, then choose Modify Length Resample Stretched Hairs from the Hair panel.

To display: Select the hair object, click the Selection button on the Select panel, and then click the Hair Resample Operator icon.

Mute

Toggles the deformation on and off without affecting the values of other parameters.
