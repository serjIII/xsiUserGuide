Get Orthogonal Basis

Returns two vectors that are perpendicular to each other as well as to the input vector.

NOTE:If you animate the input, the output vectors are not guaranteed to vary smoothly.

Categories: Vector

Output Ports: U_Tangent, V_Tangent
