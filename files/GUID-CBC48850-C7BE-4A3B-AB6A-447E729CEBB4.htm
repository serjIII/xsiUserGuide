Playing a Scene

After defining your edits, you can play a scene in the Sequence time by any of the following methods:

  • Click the Sequence Play Forwards from the Start Frame button () in the playback controls. See Playback Controls.
    TIP:Middle-click on this button to continue playing from the current frame.
  • Scrub the timeline in the Camera Sequencer Editor from start to the end frame.

You can playback animation in the Camera Sequencer in real-time. It accomplishes real-time playback by choosing the frames to display based on the elapsed time.

To play back frames in real time

Do one of the following:

  • Choose Playback Real-Time Playback.

    or

  • Toggle the All/RT button to RT.

    or

  • Press Shift+up-arrow key.
