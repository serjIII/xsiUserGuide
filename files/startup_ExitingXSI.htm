Exiting Softimage

Inevitably, after you have completed your work session in Softimage, you will have to save your work and exit. Any changes you made to your workspace layout are saved when you exit and are automatically recalled the next time you start Softimage.

To exit Softimage

  1. Do one of the following:

    • Choose File Exit from the main menu bar.

      or

    • Click the close icon (Ã—) at the far right of the title bar.

      or

    • Press Alt+F4.

      or

    • Press Ctrl+Q.

  2. If you have made any changes to the scene since the last save, you are prompted to save them before exiting. Click one of the following:

    • Yes to save your changes. For more information about saving scenes in general, see Saving Scenes [ NO LABEL ].

      or

    • No to exit without saving.

      or

    • Cancel to continue working.
