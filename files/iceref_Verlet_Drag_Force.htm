Verlet Drag Force

Applies a force that slows points down over time. This node is designed to be plugged into the Force input of Verlet Framework. See Verlet Integration [ICE Deformations].

Tasks: Deformation/Verlet Forces

Output Ports: Force

Strength

Controls how quickly the points lose velocity.
