Cameras

Virtual cameras in Softimage are similar to physical cameras in the real world. They define the views that you can render. You can create and render as many cameras as you want in a scene.

For information about navigating the camera (zoom, pan, dolly, roll, etc.), see Navigating in 3D Views [Viewing and Navigating].
