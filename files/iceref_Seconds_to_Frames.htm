Seconds to Frames

This compound converts time values from seconds to frames.

Plug its Frames output into any time-related port that accepts a 3D vector value, such as any port on the Equals compound. For example, you could get and convert the particle age from seconds into frames, make it equal to a specific value, then delete the points (particles) when they reach that value.

Category: Conversion

Output Ports: Frames

Seconds

The time value in seconds.
