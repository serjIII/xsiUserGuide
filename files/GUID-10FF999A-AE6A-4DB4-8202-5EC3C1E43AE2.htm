Displaying Shot Tracks and Track Names

You can toggle the display of Shot Tracks and their names in the Camera Sequencer Editor in any of the following ways:

  • Choose View Shot Tracks from the menu bar.
  • Choose View Preferences from the menu bar.

    In the Camera Sequencer Editor Preferences window, click the Tracks tab and select Shot Tracks and Track Name to set a preference for the display options.

  • Right-click on a Shot Track and choosing Display Options.

    This opens the Camera Sequencer Editor Preferences window with the Tracks tab visible.

  • Choose View Clear All to clear all tracks from the Camera Sequencer Editor.
    NOTE:To retrieve the cleared Shot Tracks, click the Update button () on the toolbar.
