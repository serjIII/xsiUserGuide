Add Verlet Force

Adds a force to points, with an option to specify whether it is in global or local space. This node is used internally by Add Verlet Forces.

Tasks: Deformation/Verlet Forces

Output Ports: value

Force

A vector defining the force to add.

Global Space

Controls whether Force is interpreted in global space (true) or local space (false).
