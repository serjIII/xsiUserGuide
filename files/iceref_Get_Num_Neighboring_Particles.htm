Get Num Neighboring Particles

This compound gets the amount of the particle's neighboring particles. You can output the Number of Neighbors.

Tasks: Particles/Getters

Output Ports: Number_Neighbors

Cutoff Distance

The maximum distance (in Softimage units) in which the particle considers its neighbors.
