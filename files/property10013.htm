Hair Unlock Operator Property Editor

Unlocks locked hairs on guide curves, allowing them to be affected by dynamics and styling operations.

To apply: Select one or more locked points, and then choose Modify Surf Lock Unlock from the Hair panel.

To display: Select the hair object, click the Selection button on the Select panel, and then click the Hair Unlock Operator icon.

For more information, see Locking Selected Points on Strands [ Hair ].

Mute

Toggles the deformation on and off without affecting the values of other parameters.
