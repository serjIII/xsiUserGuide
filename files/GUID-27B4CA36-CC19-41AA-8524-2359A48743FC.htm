Overriding Exposed Ports

You can override ports on ICE compounds. Each overridden port is marked with the following:

  • Override icon () appears on the overridden port and on the port layout group
  • Exposed port name is greyed out
  • Connection lines, to and from the overridden ports, appear with a different stipple pattern, and are greyed out

B

C

NOTE:You cannot override exposed ports of type String.
