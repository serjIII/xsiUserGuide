mib_texture_polkasphere

Category: mental ray > Texture

Shader Family: Texture

Output: Color

This shader draws a sphere with programmable diameter into the cube. The foreground and background colors are programmable.

See Texture Shaders for more information.

Name

The name of the shader node displayed in the render tree. Enter any name you like, or leave the default.

coord

The coordinate to texture.

radius

The radius of the sphere in the unit cube.

fgcolor

The foreground color of the sphere.

bgcolor

The background color around the sphere.
