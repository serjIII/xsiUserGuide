SCM Fixer Op Property Editor

Controls the continuity at the seams between the subsurfaces of an assembled surface mesh.

NOTE:This operator has been replaced in Softimage versions 1.5 and higher. See SCM Fixer Op2 Property Editor.

Always Evaluate

Activates continuity control at the seams. When this option is off, the operator does not fix the seams. This allows you to adjust the subsurfaces before turning this option back on again.

Continuity

The degree of continuity maintained at the seams:

  • C0 (Position). The point is joined by a straight line segment.

  • C1 (Tangent). The point is joined by a smooth curve segment and the tangent does not change abruptly.
