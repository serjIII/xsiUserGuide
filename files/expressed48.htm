Plus (+) (Expression Function Reference)

The Function Arithmetic Plus command in the expression editor inserts the math operator token + at the insertion point in the editing pane. This token adds the two sides of the equation.

Operators follow the standard of precedence: items enclosed in brackets first, then * and /, followed by + and -.
