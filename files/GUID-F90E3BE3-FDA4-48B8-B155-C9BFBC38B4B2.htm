Halting ICE Trees Evaluation

You can halt or interrupt the evaluation of one or more ICE trees in a scene by clicking the icon on the Softimage tool bar.

To interrupt the ICE tree that is currently under evaluation, click the icon in the main menu bar. To interrupt the evaluation of the current and yet to be evaluated ICE trees, press the Ctrl key and then click the icon.

NOTE:The icon is enabled only when the ICE tree evaluation time lasts longer than one second.
TIP:When visible, mouse-over on the icon to view the name of the ICE tree that is currently being evaluated.

When you interrupt the evaluation process, Softimage mutes the current and yet to be evaluated ICE trees. For more information about muting ICE trees, see Muting ICE Trees.

As soon as you interrupt the evaluation, the Unmute ICE trees icon () is enabled. Clicking this icon displays all the unevaluated and muted ICE trees.

From the list, you can choose to unmute and evaluate IEC trees one-by-one or all at once by choosing Unmute all.

TIP:You can also unmute ICE trees, one-by-one, in the Explorer by right-clicking on a muted ICE tree and toggling the Mute option.
