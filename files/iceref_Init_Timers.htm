Init Timers

This compound initializes the timer attributes that exist in the point cloud's ICE tree. This compound works in conjunction with the other Timers compounds for ICE particles.

To ensure that timers are per-particle (not per-object), plug its Execute output into an Execute on Emit port of an Emit compound.

For more information, see Setting Particle Timers.

Tasks: Particles/Timers

Output Ports: Execute
