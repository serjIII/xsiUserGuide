Greater than or Equal (>=) (Expression Function Reference)

The FunctionConditionsGreater than or Equal command in the expression editor inserts the Boolean operator token >= at the insertion point in the editing pane. This token makes the equation on its left side to be greater than or equal to the equation on its right side.
