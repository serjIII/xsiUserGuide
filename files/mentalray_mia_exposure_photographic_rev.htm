mia_exposure_photographic_rev

This shader is part of the mentay ray architectural material library. It approximately inverses the effect of the mia_exposure_photographic shader. The parameter sets of these two shaders are identical.

For general information on the mental ray shaders, see the mental ray Documentation at www.autodesk.com/mentalray-help-2013-enu.
