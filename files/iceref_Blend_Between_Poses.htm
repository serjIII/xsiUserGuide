Blend Between Poses

Blends between two poses. A pose is an array of 4x4 matrices, which represents the transforms of deformers.

TIP:To produce best results, use local transforms.

Tasks: Crowds/Animation

Output Ports: Pose

Pose 1

Array of 4x4 matrices representing the deformer transforms in Pose 1.

Pose 2

Array of 4x4 matrices representing the deformer transforms in Pose 2.

Blend

Blend factor between Pose 1 and Pose 2.
