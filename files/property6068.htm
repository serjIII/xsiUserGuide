Tetrahedron Property Editor

To get a tetrahedron, choose Get Primitive Polygon Mesh Tetrahedron. Polygon mesh objects are composed of polygons joined at their edges and vertices.

To redisplay: Select the tetrahedron then press Enter and click the Tetrahedron tab.
