Strand Drag Force

This compound applies a force that slows down particle strands over time by adding flow resistance to the movement. Unlike the Drag Force compound in the Forces group, this compound applies a force to the strands themselves, not the particles.

Plug this compound's Force output into a Force port on the Add Strand Forces compound or to a Forces port on the Strand Dynamics Framework compound.

For more information on using dynamics on strands, see Creating Strand Dynamics [ICE Particle Simulations].

Tasks: Particles/Strand Dynamics

Output Ports: Force

Strength

Defines how much drag is applied to the particle strand at each frame (how much velocity is lost by the object).
