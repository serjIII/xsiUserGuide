Geometry Property Editor (Spiral)

Controls the number of points on a primitive spiral.

To apply: Choose Get Primitive Curve Spiral.

To redisplay: Select the spiral, click the Selection button on the Select panel, then click the Geometry icon.

U Subdivisions

The number of segments. Higher subdivision values produce greater resolution and denser, heavier geometry.
