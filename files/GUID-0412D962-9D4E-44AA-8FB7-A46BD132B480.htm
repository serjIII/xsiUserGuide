Deleting ShotClips

To delete a ShotClip

  • Right-click on a ShotClip and choose Delete.

To delete multiple ShotClips

  1. Press Ctrl or Shift+select the ShotClips from one or different Short Tracks.
  2. Right-click on one of the selected ShotClips and choose Delete or press the Delete key.
NOTE:Deleting a ShotClip is affected by rippling. This controls how other ShotClips on a rippled Shot Tracks move in relation to the deleted ShotClip.
