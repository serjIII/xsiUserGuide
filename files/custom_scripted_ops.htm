Scripted Operators

Scripted operators are similar to expressions but are much more powerful. They provide a way to use scripts to control anything from a single parameter to whole parameter sets or even an object's geometry.

If you plan to reuse an operator with multiple scenes and objects, consider using self-installing custom operators instead. See Custom Operators [SDK Guide].
