dielectric_material_photon

Category: mental ray > Photon

Shader Family: Photon Material

Output: Color

This shader is used for photon tracing of objects that have the dielectric_material shader applied. It has the same parameters as the dielectric_material shader.

See dgs_material_photon for more information about photon shaders.
