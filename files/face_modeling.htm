Modeling the Head and Its Parts for Face Robot

Having a good head model is probably the most important thing you need when working with Face Robot. A good model lays the foundation for the level of results that you can achieve with Face Robot. Because of this, you should spend a good amount of time making sure that the model is the best it can be for facial animation. Remember that Face Robot is intended only for human or human-like faces.

While Face Robot is designed to be as flexible as possible in processing head models, it does make certain assumptions about facial anatomy, model placement, etc. This section outlines a number of requirements, useful practices, and guidelines that will help you to get the best possible results from your model.
