Center Property Editor

The Center property is automatically created when you select an object, activate Center mode, and perform any transformation (scaling, rotation, translation). It is also created when you freeze all or some of an object's transformations, or move the center to selected vertices.

To display: Select the object then choose Edit Properties Modeling Properties and click the Center tab.

NOTE:These parameters represent offsets from the new position of the object's center to the center of geometry. Any transformations applied to the object are applied on top of these offsets. Changing these parameters will affect the geometry, not its center.

Mute

Toggles the deformation on and off without affecting the values of other parameters.

Scaling X, Y, Z

The scaling factor of the object relative to its center.

Rotation X, Y, Z

The rotation offset of the object relative to its center.

Translation X, Y, Z

The translation offset of the object relative to its center.

Scale Orientation X, Y, Z

An extra rotation that is applied before scaling. Normally, transformations are applied in the following order: first scaling, then rotation, and finally translation.
