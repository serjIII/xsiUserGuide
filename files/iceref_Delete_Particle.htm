Delete Particle

This compound simply deletes particles.

Plug its Execute output into any compound with an Execute port, such as the Execute on Trigger or Execute Once on Enter State port in the State compound, or the Execute upon Collision port on the Bounce Off Surface compound.

For more information on deleting particles, see ICE Particle Age [ICE Particle Simulations].

Tasks: Particles/Deleting Particles

Output Ports: Execute
