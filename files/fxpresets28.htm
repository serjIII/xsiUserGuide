CMY Adjust

Adjusts the cyan, magenta and yellow values of a clip using a graph.

Requires an Input (RGB or RGBA).

Produces an Output (RGB or RGBA).

Graphs

Graphs

The cyan, magenta and yellow values of the clip are plotted as three separate lines on the same graph. To adjust a particular color, click the Cyan, Magenta, or Yellow button above the graph. The horizontal axis represents the cyan, magenta or yellow values in, and the vertical axis represents the cyan, magenta or yellow values out, as appropriate.
