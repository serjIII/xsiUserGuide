Lagoa Duplicate and Stretch

Clones points and moves them along their velocity vector.

This compound should be applied to a point cloud that is reading from cache files.

To apply: See Applying Cached Post Sim Effects [Lagoa Multiphysics Simulations].

Tasks: Lagoa/Cached Sim Post Effect

Output Ports: clone

Min Density

The minimum density for cloning points.

Distance to Master Point

Adjusts the distance of cloned points from the original points.
