Cap Hole

Tasks: Topology/Modifiers

Output Ports: Execute

Fills a hole in a polygon mesh.

To apply:

  1. Select a border vertex.

  2. Choose Modify Poly. Mesh Cap Hole from the Model toolbar.

    If you did not select a border vertex in the previous step, you are now prompted to pick a mesh and then a border vertex.

  3. Repeat to fill additional holes.

To redisplay:

  • Expand the object's construction stack in an explorer and click the Cap_Hole icon.

Enable

Toggles the effect on and off.

Border Vertex Index

The index of a point on the border to close.

Triangulate Non Planar Cap

Creates a fan of triangles from the picked vertex.
