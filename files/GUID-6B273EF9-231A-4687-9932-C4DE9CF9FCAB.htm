Camera Sequencer Editor Overview

The Camera Sequencer Editor provides tools to layout and manage ShotClips, then produce rendered movie footage of the animation in your scene.

To open the Camera Sequencer Editor in a separate window

  • From the View menu, choose Animation Camera Sequencer Editor.
