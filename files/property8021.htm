Fit Surface Op Property Editor

Creates a NURBS surface by resampling and fitting it to an existing surface. You can also generate a cubic surface from a linear one, and vice versa. The parameterization of the new surface is uniform.

To apply: Select a surface and choose Create Surf. Mesh Fit from the Model menu.

To redisplay: Select the generated surface, click the Selection button on the Select panel, then click the Fit Surface icon.

Inputs

Hide/Unhide

Hides all the inputs for the generated object to simplify the display of your scene in the 3D views, or unhides the inputs when you need to modify them.

Delete

Freezes the generator and deletes the inputs. This simplifies the scene once you are satisfied that you won't need to modify the inputs again.

U, V Subdivision

The number of segments in the corresponding directions. Higher subdivision values produce greater resolution and denser, heavier geometry.

Degree in U, V

The degree of the NURBS surface in the corresponding direction: Linear or Cubic.

Close in U, V

Closes the generated surface in the corresponding direction.

Maintain Discontinuity in U, V

Preserves discontinuities in the original surface caused by multiknots in the corresponding direction.

Note that when one of these option is on, the number of segments in the result is never less than the number of segments in the input surfaces, even if the corresponding Subdivision parameter specifies a lower value.
