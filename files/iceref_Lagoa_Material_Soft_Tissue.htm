Lagoa Material Soft Tissue

Creates a stretchy elastic material using the point cloud that is emitted. This is similar to Lagoa Material Soft Loose except that it's stretchier. The elastic structure has a little stretch and shear resistance, but more damping. Some pressure is also used to attract the particles to each other and help keep their overall shape. The surface friction is high to make the particles stick to collision objects.

The parameters exposed for this compound are a subset of those in the Lagoa Main Material compound, mainly in the Pressure and Elasticity groups.

Plug this compound's Execute output into an Execute port of the Lagoa Phase node.

For more information on setting up a Lagoa ICE tree, see Creating a Lagoa Effect from Scratch.
