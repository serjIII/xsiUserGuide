Get Current Frame Velocity

An internal compound used by the Basic Crowd Simulation to compute the actor velocity per frame.

Tasks: Crowds/Getters

Output Ports:

  • Velocity — Vector3 representing the actor velocity.

  • Speed — Scalar representing the actor speed.
