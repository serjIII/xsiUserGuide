Lagoa Material Melt

Creates an elastic structure that breaks apart into coherent "puddles" upon collision. The elasticity is high to keep the structure, as well as the pressure and plasticity values that help create the puddles after it breaks apart. The surface friction is fairly high to help the particles stick to collision objects.

The parameters exposed for this compound are all of those in the Lagoa Main Material compound, mainly in the Elasticity, Plasticity, and Pressure Settings groups.

Plug this compound's Execute output into an Execute port of the Lagoa Phase node.

For more information on setting up a Lagoa ICE tree, see Creating a Lagoa Effect from Scratch.

Tasks: Lagoa/Material Type

Output Ports: Execute
