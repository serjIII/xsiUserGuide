Clip Display Options Dialog Box (Animation Mixer)

Switches the display of various clip properties in the animation mixer. Some of these options can also be set on the animation mixer's View menu.

To display: Right-click on a clip and choose Display Options.

Name

The name displayed in the center of clips.

Effects

The yellow bar over a clip to indicate a clip effect or offset effect, or an aqua bar indicating a pose effect.

Clip in/out

In and out frame numbers relative to the source in the bottom corners of clips.

Weight Curves

Red lines representing weight curves in clips.

Scale

Scale factor displayed at the top of clips. If a clip had been scaled to twice its length, the scale factor is 0.50.

Time Warp

Orange lines representing timewarp curves in clips.

Extrap(olation) Type

Type and length of extrapolation displayed in the center of extrapolated clips: B for the number of bounces, C for the number of cycles, or H for the number of frames to hold.

Audio Wave

Black lines representing the waveform of audio files.

Cycle Marks

Vertical lines separating each cycle or bounce in extrapolated clips.

Markers

Rust-colored outlines representing markers in clips.
