SurfaceGeometricNormal

The normal of the underlying geometry at a location.

This attribute is available at surface locations only. On polygon meshes, it is the normal of the tessellated triangle and is not interpolated. On NURBS surfaces and curves, it is the exact normal at that location.

Data Type

3D vectors

Belongs To

n/a

Geometry Types

  • polygon mesh

  • NURBS surface

  • NURBS curve

Attribute Type

inherent
