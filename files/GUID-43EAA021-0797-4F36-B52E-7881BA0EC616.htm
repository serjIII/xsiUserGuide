Toolbar and Playback Controls

Toolbar

The Camera Sequencer toolbar displays some of the frequently used functions.

 

Locks a Shot Track and its contents.
 

Updates all your actions in the Camera Sequencer Editor.
 

On an empty Shot Track, adds a ShotClip at the current frame. See Adding ShotClips on a Shot Track.
 

Displays the Help for the Camera Sequencer Editor.

Playback Controls

In the Camera Sequencer layout, the playback controls are available on the Camera Sequencer Editor and below the Sequencer Camera.

Playback Control Description Shortcut Key
 

Steps back one frame in the sequence. Alt+PageUp
 

Steps forward one frame in the sequence. Alt+PageDown
 

Moves backward to the first frame in the sequence. Alt+Home
 

Stops playing the sequence. Alt+down-arrow
 

Plays the sequence.  
 

Moves forward to the end of sequence. Alt+End
NOTE:All the playback controls are also available in the Camera Sequencer Playcontrols window. To open it, choose View Toolbars Camera Sequencer Playcontrols.

Following are the additional tools available below the Sequencer Camera viewport:

 

Creates a Sequencer camera without interest. See Creating Cameras With Interest and Without Interest.
 

Creates a Sequencer camera with interest. See Creating Cameras With Interest and Without Interest.
 

Plots the animation and sequencer camera. See Plotting Sequencer Camera and Animation.
 

Resets the Camera Sequencer layout.
NOTE:All the above tools are also available in the Camera Sequencer Tools window. To open it, choose View Toolbars Camera Sequencer Tools.
