Modeling the Eyeballs

For the eyes, you need two have two eyeballs as separate polygon meshes or groups.

  • Location. The eyeballs should be in their sockets, obviously.

  • Orientation. The eyes should be looking forward towards positive Z and their line of gaze should be exactly parallel.

  • Cornea. Eyeballs should have inner and outer shell with a little bulge in the front for the cornea.

  • Eyelids and Sockets. The eye sockets should be modeled such that at least the inside of the upper and lower eyelid is present.

  • Lacrimal Gland. For realism, there should be lacrimal glands at the inside of each eye, but they're not mandatory.

TIP:To create eyelash meshes inside Face Robot, see Building Eyelash Meshes.
