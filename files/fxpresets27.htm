RGB Adjust

Adjusts the red, green and blue values of a clip using a graph.

Requires an Input (RGB or RGBA).

Produces an Output (RGB or RGBA).

Graphs

Graphs

The red, green and blue values of the clip are plotted as three separate lines on the same graph. To adjust a particular color, click the Red, Green, or Blue button above the graph. The horizontal axis represents the red, green or blue values in, and the vertical axis represents the red, green or blue values out, as appropriate.

Mask

Controls this operator's Mask Options.

Output

Controls this operator's Output Options.
