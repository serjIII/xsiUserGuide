Geometry Property Editor (Grid)

Controls the number of points on a geometric grid.

To apply: Choose Get Primitive Polygon Mesh Grid or Get Primitive Surface Grid.

To redisplay: Select the grid, click the Selection button on the Select panel, then click the Geometry icon.

Subdivisions

U, V

The number of segments in the corresponding directions. Higher subdivision values produce greater resolution and denser, heavier geometry.
