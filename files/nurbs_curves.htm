Curves

Curves are cubic or linear NURBS splines. You cannot render them, but you can use them for various purposes in Softimage.

Related Information

In addition to these procedures, you can also get primitive curves and text (as described in Starting From Scratch: Primitives and Text [Data Exchange]).
