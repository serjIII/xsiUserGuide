Square Property Editor

To get a square, choose one of the commands on the Primitive menu. Curve squares are linear NURBS curves. Implicit objects are non-renderable shapes that can be used, e.g., to define paths, constraints, etc.

To redisplay: Select the square then press Enter and click the Square tab.

Length

The length of the sides in SOFTIMAGE units.
