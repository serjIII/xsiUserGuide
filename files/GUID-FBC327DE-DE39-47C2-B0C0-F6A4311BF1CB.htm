Selecting Shot Tracks

When you select a Shot Track, all appropriate commands are applied to it, such as inserting a track or accessing Shot Track controls.

To select a Shot Track

  • Click the Shot Track icon ().

    The selected Shot Track is indicated by a white line around the Shot Track control icons area.

To select multiple Shot Tracks

  • Shift+click the Shot Track icon.

    or

  • Ctrl+click the Shot Track icon to toggle-select multiple tracks.
