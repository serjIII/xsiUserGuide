Stick To Ground

Keeps the actor position on the ground surface. This compound is useful when the terrain is uneven.

Tasks: Crowds/Behavior

Output Ports: Post Simulate. Connect this port to the Post Simulation group on the Behavior Core compound.

Ground

Additional geometry to be considered ground.
