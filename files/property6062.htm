Icosahedron Property Editor

To get an icosahedron, choose Get Primitive Polygon Mesh Icosahedron. Polygon mesh objects are composed of polygons joined at their edges and vertices.

To redisplay: Select the icosahedron then press Enter and click the Icosahedron tab.
