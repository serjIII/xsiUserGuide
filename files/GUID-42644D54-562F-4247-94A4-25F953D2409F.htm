Changing the Default Camera of a ShotClip

For each ShotClip, you need to define an active camera. By default, the first camera that is available in the scene is selected.

To change the default camera

  1. Right-click on a ShotClip, and choose Clip Properties.
  2. From the Active Camera Name drop-down list, choose a camera to make it active.
