Divide (/) (Expression Function Reference)

The Function Arithmetic Divide command in the expression editor inserts the math operator token / at the insertion point in the editing pane. This token divides the two sides of the equation.

Operators follow the standard of precedence: items enclosed in brackets first, then * and /, followed by + and -.
