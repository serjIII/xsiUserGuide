Locking ShotClips

You can lock ShotClips on the same or different Shot Tracks to prevent them from being moved, scaled, or rippled. This locks the Time Control property of ShotClips and turns them to a wine color.

To lock ShotClips

  • Right-click on a ShotClip and choose Toggle Lock.

    or

  • Select one or more ShotClips and choose Clip Toggle Lock.

To unlock ShotClips

  • Right-click on a ShotClip and choose Toggle Lock again.

    or

  • Select one or more ShotClips and choose Clip Toggle Lock again.
NOTE:Whether the ShotClips are locked or not, you cannot move more than one ShotClip to another Shot Track.
