Arc Property Editor

To get an arc, choose one of the commands on the Primitive menu. Curve objects are cubic NURBS curves. Implicit objects are non-renderable shapes that can be used, e.g., to define paths, constraints, etc.

To redisplay: Select the arc then press Enter and click the Arc tab.

Radius

The radius of the arc in SOFTIMAGE units.

Start, End Angle

The start and end positions of the arc in degrees. The arc is drawn from the start angle to the end angle.
