Scripting Crosswalk for Max

You can script the export and import of dotXSI files to and from a Crosswalk-enabled installation of 3ds Max.

Exporting 

exportFile "C:/temp/test.xsi" #noprompt

Importing 

importFile "C:/temp/test.xsi" #noprompt
