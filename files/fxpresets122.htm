Interlace

Interlaces the fields of a sequence. The dominant field of the first frame is interlaced with the other field of the second frame, the dominant field of the third frame with the other field of the fourth frame and so on. This produces a sequence of half the original length.

Requires an Input (Any).

Produces an Output (Any).

Source Clip Range
