Lagoa Setup Pouring Liquid from Volume

Emits Lagoa particles from a volume.

Tasks: Lagoa/Presets

Output Ports: Execute. Connect this port to a Simulated ICE tree.

Sim Quality

Specify the simulation quality. The smaller the value, finer particles are generated.

Emitter In Name

Name of the emitting geometry

Color

Particle color

Optimize

Option to optimize the Accuracy and Speed

Initial Force (X, Y, Z)

Initial force of the particle

Passive Body In Name

Name of geometry with which the particles collide

Surface Tension

Surface tension of the liquid particles. The higher the surface value, the thicker the liquid behaves.

Internal Pressure

Internal pressure in the liquid

External Pressure

External pressure on the liquid

Gravity

Gravity of the particles

Density

Density of the particles

Force1 (X, Y, Z)

Additional force applied on the particles
