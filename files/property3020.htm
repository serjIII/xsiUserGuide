Transition Property Editor

Sets the transition weight and type of the current transition. The settings here are also used when you choose Mix Auto Transition in the mixer.

For more information, see Creating Transitions between Clips [Nonlinear Animation in the Animation Mixer].

To display: Right-click on a transition in the animation mixer and choose Properties.

Active

Toggles the activeness of the transition.

Transition Weight

The value of the transition weight curve at the current frame.

Transition Type

The spatial interpolation method of the transition:

  • Standard creates a direct, linear transition from one source to the next.

  • Cardinal creates a smooth transition that interpolates between the sources of up to the four nearest clips.

  • Bridge processes the matching parameters between actions and creates a separate curve for each relevant parameter (such as the XYZ parameters of a translation).
