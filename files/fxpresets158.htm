Film Mask

Creates a black mask that is overlaid on the image to produce letterbox type effects.

Requires a Foreground (Any).

Produces an Output (Any).

Film Mask

Envelope Ratio

Specifies the extent to which the mask covers the image on the vertical axis. Increasing this value grows the mask from the top and bottom of the image.

Extraction Ratio

Specifies the extent to which the mask covers the image on the horizontal axis. Increasing this value grows the mask from the left and right of the image.

Mask

Controls this operator's Mask Options.

Output

Controls this operator's Output Options.
