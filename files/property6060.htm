Dodecahedron Property Editor

To get a dodecahedron, choose Get Primitive Polygon Mesh Dodecahedron. Polygon mesh objects are composed of polygons joined at their edges and vertices.

To redisplay: Select the dodecahedron then press Enter and click the Dodecahedron tab.
