Offset Particle from Emitter

This compound offsets the particle from its emit location when it's born, as defined by the particle's EmitLocation attribute.

Plug this compound's Execute on Emit output into an Execute on Emit port of an Emit compound.

For more information, see Offsetting the Particles from the Emitter [ICE Particle Simulations].

Tasks: Particles/Emission Control

Output Ports: Execute_on_Emit

Distance

The distance (in Softimage units) from the emit location at which the particle is born.
