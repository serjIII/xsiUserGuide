Realtime Shaders

You can use realtime shaders to achieve a multitude of sophisticated rendering effects in Autodesk® Softimage® from basic surface shading to complex texture blending and reflection. You build and control the realtime rendering pipeline by connecting shader nodes in the render tree and displaying the effects in any 3D view set to a realtime OpenGL or DirectX9 display mode.
