Using WeightMap, Points, and StiffnessMap Ports in Syflex Cloth

Using WeightMap and Points to Vary Deformations

The WeightMap and Points ports are available in the Syflex Cloth ICE compound to define and control the points of a cloth influenced by he simulation.

  • WeightMap — The per-point values in the map, usually between 0 and 1, control how much the points of the cloth are influenced by the simulation. 0 means that the point is not modified by the simulation.

  • Points — Defines which points are used in the simulation.

Using StiffnessMap to Vary Deformation

The StiffnessMap modifies the stiffness of each spring of the cloth that are attached to this point. The StiffnessMap is connected to the three input ports, StretchMap, ShearMap, and BendMap in the Syflex Cloth Springs compound.
