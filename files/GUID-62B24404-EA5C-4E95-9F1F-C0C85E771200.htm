Deleting Shot Tracks

Deleting a Shot Track removes all ShotClips it may contain.

TIP:You can always undo (Ctrl+Z) to retrieve the deleted Shot Tracks.

To delete Shot Tracks

  • Right-click the track you want to delete and choose Delete Track. This deletes only the track on which you right-click.

    or

  • Select one or more tracks by clicking (Shift+click for multiple tracks), then choose Track Remove Track from the menu bar or press Delete.

To delete empty Shot Tracks

  • Choose Track Delete Unused from the menu bar.

    This removes all Shot Tracks that do not contain ShotClips on them.
