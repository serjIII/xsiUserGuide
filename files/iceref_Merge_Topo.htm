Merge Topo

Categories

  • Topology

Description

Creates a new topology by merging several topologies together.

Ports

Inputs

Topo

A topology to merge. Add more topologies by connecting them to New (Topo).

If the first Topo port is unconnected, the self's topology is used. The other topologies will be merged into the self, and the self will still retain all its attributes.

Outputs

Result

The new topology.
