syflexIVolume

Categories

  • Syflex Force

Description

Applies a pressure force that increases or decreases the overall volume of a polygon mesh object. This allows you to create inflation and deflation effects on a cloth object, making it behave like a soft body. The force is applied along the cloth object's normals (perpendicular to the faces) and is proportional to the volume of the object.

This node is not supported for Syflex curves because they have no faces.

This node is the same as the Syflex Volume compound.

For information on Syflex ICE forces, see Syflex ICE Forces.

Ports

Inputs

Pressure

The amount of pressure, or intensity, of the force. Increasing this value inflates the polygon mesh object.

Volume

The volume of the polygon mesh object. A value is 1 is the volume of the object in its presimulated state.

Damp

The damp coefficient, which controls the intensity of the damping effect on the simulated object's movement.

Outputs

Force

The output force.
