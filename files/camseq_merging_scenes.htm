Merging Scenes

You can bring in a Camera Sequencer from one scene to another by merging the scenes. This means that the Shot Tracks and ShotClips from the merged scene are added to an existing Camera Sequencer.

To merge scenes

  1. Open a scene in the Camera Sequencer.
  2. Choose File Merge from the main menu.
  3. In the browser that appears, locate and select the Softimage scene that you want to merge, then click OK.

    Shot Tracks from the merged scene are added to an existing Camera Sequencer, and appear below the already existing Shot Tracks.

    All merged Shot Tracks and ShotClips are labeled with the prefix Merged.

For more information on merging scenes in Softimage, see Merging Scenes.
