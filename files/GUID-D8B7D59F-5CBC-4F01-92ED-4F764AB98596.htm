Renaming a ShotClip

By default, the name of the first ShotClip that you add on any Short Track is Shot. The subsequent names of ShotClips that you add are appended with numbers starting from 1 (Shot1, Shot2, and so on).

To rename a ShotClip

  1. Open the Explorer (press 8).
  2. Expand the Camera_Sequencer Tracks CameraShot Shot_Track Clip list folder.
  3. Right-click on the ShotClip name and choose Rename.
