Camera Sequencer

This section explains the components in the Camera Sequencer tool, and its benefits in the animation workflow.

The Camera Sequencer provides you an easy and fast way to create Pre-visualization, Animatics, and Cinematics. Using the Camera Sequencer, you can:

  • Rearrange the original animation in time â€” similar to an editing tool
  • Define a camera for a specific time range

The Camera Sequencer contains three major components:

A Camera Sequencer Editor
B Viewports that display the Sequencer, Front, and User camera views
C Playback controls, tools to create cameras based on interest and plot sequencer cameras

To view the Camera Sequencer layout

  • From the View menu, choose Layouts Camera Sequencer.
