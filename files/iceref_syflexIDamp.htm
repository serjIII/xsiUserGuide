syflexIDamp

Categories

  • Syflex Force

Description

Applies a damping force that slows down the movement of the simulated cloth or curve object by dissipating its energy. The intensity of the damp force is proportional to the simulated object's velocity.

This node is used in the Syflex Cloth and Syflex Curve compounds, and is the same as the Syflex Damp compound.

For information on Syflex ICE forces, see Syflex ICE Forces.

Ports

Inputs

Damp

The damp coefficient, which controls the intensity of the damping effect.

Outputs

Force

The output force.
