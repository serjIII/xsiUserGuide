Displaying ShotClips Information

You can toggle display the ShotClip labels (Name, Clip In and Out, and Scale) by turning them on or off in the Camera Sequencer Editor Preferences window.

To open the Camera Sequencer Editor Preferences window

  • Choose View Preferences from the Camera Sequencer Editor menu bar.

    or

  • Right-click anywhere on a Shot Track or ShotClip and choose Display Options.

    The Clips tab contains options to display the ShotClip labels.
