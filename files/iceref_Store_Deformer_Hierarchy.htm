Store Deformer Hierarchy

Stores the hierarchical data of deformers into the self.CrowdFX_Deformers_ParentIndices ICE attribute. The hierarchical data is required to convert the deformer transforms from local space to global or vice versa.

Tasks: Crowds/Animation

Output Ports: Execute

In Name

Name of the deformer in the deformer proxy.
