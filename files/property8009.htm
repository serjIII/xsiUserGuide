Remove Curve Knot Op Property Editor

Removes a knot from a NURBS curve.

To apply: Select a knot on a curve, then choose Modify Curve Remove Knot from the Model menu.

To redisplay: Select the curve, click Selection on the Select panel, then click the corresponding Remove crv knot op icon.

Multiplicity

The multiplicity to remove from the knot. If you remove all of a knot's multiplicity, the knot itself is removed. If a knot has multiplicity greater than 1 and you remove only part of it, the continuity increases.
