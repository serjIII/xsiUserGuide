Changing the Scene Time and Sequence Time of a ShotClip

You can change the Scene Time and Sequence Time range of one or more ShotClips by specifying their first and last frames in the Time Control property editor.

To view the Time Control window

  • Right-click on a ShotClip and choose Time Properties.

    Press Ctrl or Shift+click to select multiple ShotClips.
