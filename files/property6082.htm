Surface UV Swap Property Editor

Swaps the U and V directions of a surface.

To apply: Select a surface then choose Modify Surface Swap UV from the Model toolbar.

To redisplay: Select the surface, click the Selection button on the Select panel, then click the Surface UV Swap icon.

Swap UVs

Swaps the U and V directions. When on, the original V direction becomes U and the original U direction becomes negative V, preserving the direction of the normals.
