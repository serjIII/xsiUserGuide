Get Particle Mass

This compound gets the particle's current mass data as set by its Mass attribute. You can output the Mass as scalar data.

For more information on mass, see ICE Particle Mass [ICE Particle Simulations].

Tasks: Particles/Getters

Output Ports: Mass

Reference

The particle's Mass attribute.
