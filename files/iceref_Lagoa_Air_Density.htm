Lagoa Air Density

This force takes the difference between the air force field (gravity or any other forces you've defined in the simulation) and the element's own velocity field. This difference is then used to dampen the motion of the points, which models the general air field against the element's velocity field.

Tasks: Lagoa/Forces

Output Ports: Force

Density

The dampening of the motion of the points based on the difference between the air force field and the element's own velocity field.

Threshold

Sets a limit to the air density value that can be used.
