Circle Property Editor

To get a circle, choose one of the commands on the Primitive menu. Curve objects are cubic NURBS curves. Implicit objects are non-renderable shapes that can be used, e.g., to define paths, constraints, etc.

To redisplay: Select the circle then press Enter and click the Circle tab.

Radius

The radius of the circle in SOFTIMAGE units.
