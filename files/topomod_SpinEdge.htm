Spin Edge Op Property Editor

"Rotates" edges by cycling their endpoints around the vertices of their two adjacent polygons.

     
NOTE:To turn internal edges instead of real edges, see Controlling Polygon Tessellation.

To apply:

  1. Select some edges.

  2. Choose Modify Poly. Mesh Spin Edges.

    To continue cycling, choose the command again or open the SpinEdge Op property editor and adjust the Spin value.

To redisplay

  • Select the polygon mesh object then choose Selection and click the SpinEdge Op icon.

Spin

The number of times to cycle the endpoints. Negative values cycle in the opposite direction.
