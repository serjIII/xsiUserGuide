Scene Rendering Options

The scene render options allow you to modify basic rendering-related settings that apply to every pass in your scene. Many of these options are described in this section, though some are described elsewhere in the documentation. However, you can always find descriptions for all of the scene render options in the Scene Render Options Property Editor.

In addition to the topics in this section, there are some other procedures that involve setting rendering options:

  • Setting scene-level motion blur options. See Scene Motion Blur Settings [Cameras and Motion Blur]

  • Defining the render channels that you can output in your render passes. See Render Channels & Framebuffers
