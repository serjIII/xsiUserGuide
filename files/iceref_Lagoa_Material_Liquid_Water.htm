Lagoa Material Liquid Water

Creates a basic water using the point cloud that is emitted. The internal pressure and surface tension settings are fairly high, making the particles cling together as water molecules do. The viscosity is fairly high, but not so much that it appears as a thick liquid. There is also a slight offset built in so that the particles don't get too close to a collision object.

The parameters exposed for this compound are a subset of those in the Lagoa Main Material compound, mainly in the Pressure, Viscosity, and Inelastic Settings groups.

Plug this compound's Execute output into an Execute port of the Lagoa Phase node.

For more information on setting up a Lagoa ICE tree, see Creating a Lagoa Effect from Scratch.
