Renaming a Shot Track

By default, the name of the first Shot Track that you add is Shot_Track. The subsequent names of Shot Tracks that you add or insert in the Camera Sequencer Editor are appended with numbers starting from 1 (Shot_Track1, Shot_Track2, and so on).

To rename a Shot Track

  1. Open the Explorer (press 8).
  2. Expand the Camera_Sequencer Tracks CameraShot folder.
  3. Right-click on the Shot Track name and select Rename.

When you change the name in the Explorer, you will see the Shot Track in the Camera Sequencer Editor update to reflect the new name.
