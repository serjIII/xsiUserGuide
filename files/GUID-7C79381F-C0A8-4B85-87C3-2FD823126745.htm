ShotClip

A ShotClip defines:

  • the mapping between the Sequence Time and Scene Time
  • an active camera for its duration

ShotClips are represented by boxes on Shot Tracks in the Camera Sequencer Editor. You can move, scale, copy, trim, and ripple ShotClips. ShotClips define the range of frames over which the items in the source are active and play back.

A ShotClip start frame on the Sequencer timeline.
B ShotClip end frame on the Sequencer timeline.
C ShotClip start frame on the Global timeline.
D ShotClip end frame on the Global timeline.
E Time scale of the ShotClip. A value of 1 means that it is the same scale as its source.
F Duration of the ShotClip in frames.
G ShotClip name
