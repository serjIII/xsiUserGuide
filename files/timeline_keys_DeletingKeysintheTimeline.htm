Deleting Keys in the Timeline

Deleting keys removes the keys you have selected in the timeline, either a single key or a region of keys. Deleting does not put the keys in the clipboard buffer as does cutting, but you can undo deleting keys like anything else in Softimage by pressing Ctrl+Z.

  1. Press Shift and drag in the timeline to create a region of keys.

    or

    Press Shift and click once on a single keyframe.

  2. Right-click on a region and choose Delete Keys.

    IMPORTANT:Don't press the Delete key! This deletes the selected object, not the keys in the timeline.
