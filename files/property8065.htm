Raise Crv Degree Op Property Editor

Raises the degree of curves.

To apply: Select one or more curves, then choose Modify Curve Raise Degree from the Model toolbar.

To redisplay: Select the curve, click the Selection button on the Select panel, then click the Raise Crv Degree Op icon.

Degree

The degree of the curve: 2 for quadratic or 3 for cubic.
