Sphere Property Editor

To get a sphere, choose one of the commands on the Primitive menu. Polygon Mesh objects are composed of polygons joined at their edges and vertices. Surface objects are cubic NURBS patches. Implicit objects are non-renderable shapes that can be used, e.g., to define bounding volumes for envelopes, particles, etc.

To redisplay: Select the sphere then press Enter and click the Sphere tab.
