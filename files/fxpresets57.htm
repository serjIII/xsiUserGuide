Grain Composite

Composites grain or noise into an image. The grain or noise will usually have been extracted from the original image using a Degrain (film) or Video Noise Removal (video) operator, prior to any intermediate processing; this prevents the grain or noise from becoming distorted during processing.

Requires an Image (RGBA) and a Grain (RGB).

Produces an Output (RGBA).

Composite

Mix Level

Sets the proportion of grain added to the original image.

Mask

Controls this operator's Mask Options.

Output

Controls this operator's Output Options.
